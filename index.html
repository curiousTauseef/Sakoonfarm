About the farm

Our farmhouse is situated in a traditional village in eastern India, about 22 kilometres from Bhubaneswar city. It has been constructed from scratch on agricultural land lending an air of village tranquility and aesthetics. The farmhouse contains rooms, bathroom and kitchen surrounded by natural habitat local to the region. We assure you - fresh and clean village air, free from city noise and traditional food served in banana leaves.

Feast for your senses and well-being

Entertain yourself with the sights and sounds of trees rustling in the wind and the variety of local birds. A feeling of being in a forest amidst paddy plantations, providing a remarkable farm experience.
